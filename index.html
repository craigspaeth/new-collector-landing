Why Primer?

Collecting art is a pleasure
Buying the right piece is confusing

Primer helps you become an informed art collector quickly and on your schedule. Primer pairs you with a dedicated Artsy Specialist for six-weeks of guided support through your first art acquisition. All for $550.

How it works

  • Discover

    Regardless of your art experience, proprietary Art Genome data and dedicated Artsy Specialist will guide you to art that resonate with you.

  • Connect

    Get an insider playbook to buying at galleries, fairs and auctions paired with access to experienced Artsy Specialists.

  • Participate

    Get alerted to nearby shows and events that have work most relevant to your taste. Enjoy VIP access to select events.
